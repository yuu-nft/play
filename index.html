Faq

We're here to answer any questions you have about The Humanoids! Here are some of the most frequently asked questions:
How can I get a Humanoid
Humanoids will be available for purchase on our website through an initial sale. At the time of purchase, a randomly selected Humanoid will be minted on the blockchain and delivered to your wallet and OpenSea account.
How do I actually purchase a Humanoid
The easiest way is to install a MetaMask extension in your browser and link your crypto wallet to it. Ensure you have enough ETH in your wallet to cover the cost of a Humanoid plus the associated gas fees. Approve the desired transaction on MetaMask and you are all set.
What will be the price of a Humanoid?
The pricing model is flat Rate throughout the entire sale. No FOMO curve or escalating prices. The cost of each Humanoid is set at 0.08 ETH.
What can I do with my Humanoid?
You can login to the YUU World and live digital lives, get jobs, friends, relationships, anythong really.
How do I contact the team?
Jump into our discord. All direct communication will happen there or on Twitter for now.
